🏆 PRINCIPLES FOR TRANSFORMATION
📈
Progressive Overload
Add weight or reps every week. If you lifted 20kg for 8 reps last week, aim for 20kg × 9 reps or 22.5kg × 8 reps this week.
🥩
High Protein: 120–130g/day
Chicken, eggs, paneer, dal, curd, whey. Without protein, your muscles cannot grow no matter how hard you train.
😴
7–8 Hours Sleep
Testosterone and growth hormone peak during deep sleep. Cutting sleep cuts your gains. Non-negotiable.
💧
3–4 Litres Water Daily
Dehydration drops strength by 10–15%. Drink 500ml before your workout, sip through the session.
📝
Log Every Session
If you don't track weight + reps, you can't know if you're progressing. Use the Excel tracker every single session.
🎯
Form Before Weight
One injury can set you back 4–8 weeks. Master form first, especially on Squat, Deadlift, and Bench Press.

🏃 POST-WEIGHT CARDIO GUIDE
Always do cardio AFTER weights — not before. Doing cardio first burns glycogen you need for heavy lifting and reduces strength by up to 20%.
