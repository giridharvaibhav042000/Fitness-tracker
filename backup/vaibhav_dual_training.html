🏃 POST-GYM CARDIO — Always after weights, before leaving gym
Golden Rule: Cardio goes AFTER weights, not before. You need all your glycogen for heavy lifting. Post-workout cardio burns fat when glycogen is already low — maximum fat burning effect. Keep it 10–15 min only; longer breaks down muscle you just built.

📈 6-Week Dual Progression Plan
Week 1
Foundation
Gym: 3×10–12
Cali: Learn moves
Pull: Dead hangs only
Week 2
Build Base
Gym: 4×8–12, +weight
Cali: Add negatives
Push-ups: aim 15
Week 3
Volume Up
Gym: 4–5×8–10
Cali: +1 set each
Try first pull-up
Week 4
Deload
Gym: 3×12–15 (–20%)
Cali: 50% volume
Focus on form
Week 5
Intensity
Gym: 4–5×6–10 heavy
Cali: Full pull-ups
Dips unassisted
Week 6
Peak
Gym: Max effort 6–8
Cali: 5+ pull-ups
10+ bar dips
