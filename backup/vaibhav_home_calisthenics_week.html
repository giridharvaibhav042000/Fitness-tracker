💡 TRAINING TIPS
🔥
Warm-Up Always
5-10 min jumping jacks, arm circles, leg swings, and bodyweight squats before every session.
💪
Progressive Overload
Can't add weight? Add reps, sets, reduce rest, or use harder variations (archer push-ups, one-arm variations).
🎯
Form Over Reps
Quality > Quantity. 10 perfect push-ups beat 20 sloppy ones. Control every rep, full range of motion.
⏱️
Rest Properly
Follow the prescribed rest times. Too short = fatigue, too long = workout drags. Use phone timer.
🍽️
Eat Enough Protein
120-130g protein daily. Calisthenics still breaks down muscle—you need protein to rebuild stronger.
😴
Recovery Matters
Sleep 7-8 hours. Take Sunday seriously—light activity only. Muscles grow during rest, not training.
✅ Pull-Up Bar Alternatives No pull-up bar? Use sturdy door frame, playground equipment, tree branch, or squat rack. For Australian pull-ups, use table edge or two chairs with broomstick. Can't do pull-ups yet? Do negatives (jump up, lower slowly) and assisted variations.
📈 PROGRESSION GUIDELINES
🎯 How to Progress Each Week

Week 1: Follow the prescribed reps. Focus on learning proper form.

Week 2: Add +2 reps to each exercise OR reduce rest by 10 seconds.

Week 3: Add +1 set to compound movements (push-ups, pull-ups, squats).

Week 4+: Progress to harder variations:

  • Push-Ups → Archer Push-Ups → One-Arm Push-Ups
  • Pull-Ups → Wide Grip → Archer Pull-Ups → One-Arm Progressions
  • Dips → Ring Dips → Weighted Dips (backpack with books)
  • Squats → Pistol Squat Progressions
  • Plank → Dragon Flag → Front Lever Progressions
🔄 WHEN TO USE THIS PROGRAM
✈️
Travel / Vacation
Perfect for hotel rooms or anywhere. Maintain your gains while traveling. No excuses!
🏖️
Gym Closed
Holidays, maintenance, or emergencies. Keep training consistency when gym isn't accessible.
💰
Budget Week
Skip gym membership renewal week or save money. Effective training costs zero rupees.
🤒
Light Deload
After heavy gym cycles, use this as active recovery week. Lower intensity, stay active.
🎯
Skill Building
Focus on calisthenics skills like handstands, L-sits, muscle-ups without gym distractions.
🔥
Extra Conditioning
Add this on top of gym work for extra cardio and endurance (advanced only).
⚠️ Important Notes
  • This is NOT a replacement for your regular gym program long-term. Weights build more muscle.
  • Use for 1-2 weeks max when gym isn't available, then return to your dual training system.
  • Calisthenics maintains strength but won't build as much size as progressive weight training.
  • Perfect for maintaining gains during short breaks, travel, or gym closures.
  • Can be used as Week 4 deload in your 6-week program for active recovery.
