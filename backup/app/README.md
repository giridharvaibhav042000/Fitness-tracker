# 🏋️ Vaibhav Fitness Tracker

A comprehensive Progressive Web App for tracking your 6-week dual training system combining morning gym workouts and evening calisthenics.

## Features

### ✅ Complete Workout Tracking
- **6-Day Split Program**: Monday through Saturday with specific workouts
- **Morning Gym Sessions**: Heavy compound lifts and isolation exercises
- **Evening Calisthenics**: Bodyweight skills and conditioning
- **Post-Workout Cardio**: Prescribed cardio with duration and intensity

### 📊 Progress Monitoring
- **Personal Records**: Automatic PR detection for every exercise
- **Body Measurements**: Track weight and body composition weekly
- **Workout History**: Complete log of all training sessions
- **Streak Counter**: Stay motivated with consecutive training days

### 🍽️ Nutrition Tracking
- **Daily Macros**: Track protein, carbs, and fats
- **Meal Logging**: Quick-add meals with automatic macro calculation
- **Pre-loaded Food Database**: Common Indian foods with accurate macros
- **Progress Bars**: Visual feedback on daily nutrition targets

### 🎥 Exercise Library
- **68 Exercises**: All exercises from your training program
- **YouTube Integration**: Watch form videos for every exercise
- **Muscle Groups**: See which muscles each exercise targets
- **Coaching Notes**: Sets, reps, and rest periods for each exercise

### ⏱️ Built-in Rest Timer
- **Auto-start**: Timer starts automatically after completing a set
- **Vibration Alerts**: Get notified when rest period ends
- **Customizable**: Each exercise has its own prescribed rest time

### 📱 Progressive Web App
- **Install on Phone**: Add to home screen like a native app
- **Offline Access**: Works without internet connection
- **Fast & Responsive**: Optimized for mobile devices
- **No App Store**: No need to download from Play Store or App Store

## How to Use

### Installation

1. **Open in Browser**
   - Navigate to the `app` folder
   - Open `index.html` in your web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)

2. **Install as App (Recommended)**
   - **On Android**: Tap menu (⋮) → "Add to Home Screen"
   - **On iPhone**: Tap share (□↑) → "Add to Home Screen"
   - **On Desktop**: Look for install icon in address bar

### Daily Workflow

1. **Start Your Day**
   - Open the app and check today's workout
   - Review exercises and watch form videos if needed

2. **During Gym Session**
   - Log weight and reps for each set
   - Mark sets as complete with the ✓ button
   - Use built-in rest timer between sets
   - Watch YouTube videos for form guidance

3. **Evening Calisthenics**
   - Switch to "Calisthenics" tab
   - Follow your bodyweight routine
   - Log all sets and reps

4. **Track Nutrition**
   - Log meals throughout the day
   - Monitor protein, carbs, and fats
   - Hit your macro targets (125g protein, 240g carbs, 70g fats)

5. **Review Progress**
   - Check your personal records
   - View workout streak
   - Track body weight changes

## 6-Week Progression

- **Week 1**: Foundation (3-4 sets, 10-12 reps, learn form)
- **Week 2**: Build Strength (4 sets, 8-12 reps, add weight)
- **Week 3**: Increase Volume (4-5 sets, 8-10 reps)
- **Week 4**: Deload (3 sets, 12-15 reps, -20% weight)
- **Week 5**: Intensify (4-5 sets, 6-10 reps, heavy)
- **Week 6**: Peak (4-5 sets, 6-8 reps, max effort)

## Tips for Success

### ⭐ Compulsory Exercises
- Exercises marked with ⭐ are MUST-DO exercises
- Never skip these foundational movements
- Optional exercises can be skipped if tired

### 📈 Progressive Overload
- Try to increase weight or reps each week
- The app will notify you when you hit a new PR
- Small improvements add up over 6 weeks

### 🥗 Nutrition Priority
- Hit your protein target EVERY DAY (125g minimum)
- Eat 1 hour before gym (banana + eggs)
- Post-workout meal within 45 minutes (most important)

### 💤 Recovery
- Get 7-8 hours of sleep
- Sunday is complete rest
- Deload week 4 is crucial for recovery

### 📱 Data Backup
- Your data is stored in your browser
- Don't clear browser data/cache
- For safety, take screenshots of PRs weekly

## Troubleshooting

**Videos not playing?**
- Check internet connection
- YouTube may be blocked on some networks
- Try opening video in YouTube app directly

**Lost my data?**
- Don't clear browser cache/cookies
- Reinstalling the app may clear data
- Use same browser consistently

**App not installing?**
- Some browsers don't support PWA installation
- Try using Chrome or Edge instead
- Can still use from browser directly
